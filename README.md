Career Forge is an all-in-one career development platform designed to help students, freshers, and professionals enhance their career prospects. It combines resume optimization, interview preparation, job search, and personalized skill-building roadmaps to provide a seamless career advancement experience.

Whether you are preparing for your first job or looking to upskill for better opportunities, Career Forge empowers you to take control of your career growth.

Features

Career Forge consists of four main modules:

1. Resume Analyzer üìù

The Resume Analyzer evaluates your resume and gives actionable insights to improve its effectiveness:

Detects formatting issues and provides suggestions to make your resume visually appealing.

Highlights missing keywords to make your resume ATS-friendly (Applicant Tracking System).

Evaluates clarity, grammar, and impact of your experience descriptions.

Provides recommendations on improving sections like skills, projects, and achievements.


2. Mock Interview üé§

Prepare for technical and HR interviews with realistic mock sessions:

Simulates live interviews with commonly asked questions in technical and behavioral rounds.

Provides personalized feedback on answers, communication style, and problem-solving approach.

Tracks performance over multiple sessions to monitor improvement.

Includes domain-specific mock interviews (e.g., IT, Data Science, Product Management).


3. Job Finder üîç

Search and explore career opportunities tailored to your skills and preferences:

Browse job openings from multiple sources in one place.

Filter jobs by location, experience, company, and industry.

Save favorite jobs and receive notifications about new opportunities.

Includes analytics to show the most relevant roles based on your profile and skills.


4. Roadmap üìà

Follow structured learning paths to become job-ready and track your skill development:

Curated roadmaps for different career paths (Software Development, Data Science, Cybersecurity, etc.).

Step-by-step guidance including courses, tutorials, and practical projects.

Tracks your progress and suggests the next learning milestone.

Helps users identify skill gaps and focus on high-priority areas.
